Focaccia with Caramemelized Onions,
Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

featured in:
fresh cranberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingriedients

    For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan

    For the toppings

  • 3 medium yellow onios, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushin on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (srape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medim-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all
    the dough ingredients until the fluor is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the
    dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
